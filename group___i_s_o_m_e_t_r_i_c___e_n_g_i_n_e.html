Height-aware isometric map with terrain and height layers.

Extends the legacy MAP concept with per-cell height values, configurable projection, and terrain transition support.

Applied as additional multiply on top of height tinting. Default: (1.0, 1.0, 1.0) = no change. Set by client lighting system each frame.
